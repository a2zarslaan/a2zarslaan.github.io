Weawe

Social Commerce Platform / Mobile / Desktop / Delhi



Live Preview

Weawe is a Social networking site with integrated E-Commerce that let's the users shop while socialising and earn cash back for helping others in purchase decisions. Weawe is a networking destination built around fashion, tech and lifestyle products where you can hang out and exchange thoughts & ideas, express yourself through the products you love and get answer to all your product related doubts and queries. The only social media platform that is Fun, Exciting and rewarding at the same time.


Responsibilities and Achievements:
-Planned and executed online marketing strategy. including email campaign management, social media strategy and online brand awareness.
- Performed public relations activities, such as articles and newsletters for the internet, as well as crafting content for the website.
- Improved brand awareness by creating and managing social media channels and corporate blog.
- Designing all the visual elements on the website and the Facebook page(logo, posters, banners etc.)
- Contributing to website design: Mockup designs, usability testing and user interface.
