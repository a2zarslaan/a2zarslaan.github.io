Apricity

Microtales Page / Collaborative Project / Bhopal



Live Preview

Apricity is a Facebook page dedicated to short tales based on a wide range of topics. The tales are posted on daily basis and could be tales written by admins or tales submitted by followers.


Responsibilities and Achievements:
- Designing all the visual elements of the Facebook page(logo, cover picture, banners, tales etc.)
- Working on the website aiming at an enjoyable user experience for people interested in reading these short tales.
