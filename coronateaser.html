Annual Techno Cultural Fest / BPIT / GGSIPU / Delhi



Live Preview

Corona is the Annual Techno-Cultural festival of Bhagwan Parshuram Institute of Technology(Affiliated to GGSIPU), Delhi. Loud music, tapping feet, excitement and an enthusiastic crowd is the general scene at Corona. This vibrant Techno-Cultural Festival was initiated with an aim of providing opportunities to the student community to exercise their Gray Cells while showcasing their immense talent in the cultural activities as well.


Responsibilities and Achievements:
- Created and edited the teaser video for Corona 2K16. The teaser consists of real footage of previous iterations of Corona.
