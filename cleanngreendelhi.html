Clean 'N' Green Delhi

Non Government Organisation / Self Funded / Delhi



Live Preview

Clean 'N' Green Delhi is a self funded voluntary organisation aiming to make Delhi a much cleaner city. Consisting of mostly college students, the organisation picks a dirty spot in the locality each weekend, and then clean it up on the weekend. Each clean up session is known as a SPOTFIX. Such spotfixes include cleaning up the walls and the surrounding area, then painting and designing the wall.
